About Us

The department of Computer Science &Engineering offers B.Tech degree course with an approved intake of 90 students per year and two M.Tech courses with an approved intake of 36 students. The students passing out have been decently placed for employment in a number of companies. Every year, a few students, after acquiring B.Tech degree go for higher studies in Computer related areas and management courses. The Institute through the department maintains membership of Computer Society of India(CSI). The department, in association with CSI, industries and other professional bodies organizes Technical and academic programs.
